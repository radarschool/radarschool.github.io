What this is

Research services and uncompromising instruction

Radar School provides specialised research services and direct instruction in radar systems, remote sensing, and advanced electromagnetics. The aim is to move past surface level trivia toward the actual equations, hardware architectures, and signal processing needed to build and deploy real sensor solutions, and the instruction goes as deep as the mathematics and the physics allow.

Every topic is worked through five ways, namely theory, examples, applications, code, and hardware.
